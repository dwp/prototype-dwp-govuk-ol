What you can do with this service

You can use this service to:

  • check how much you have left to pay
  • change the amount of future payments
  • find help and support with your repayments

Creating your account

You’ll need access to your:

  • personal email address
  • mobile phone or an authenticator app
  • National Insurance number

Proving your identity

You’ll also need to prove your identity. You’ll need:

  • a valid photo ID, like your passport or driving licence
  • details of your home address for the past 5 years

How to access the service

Go to https://www.gov.uk/repay-manage-benefit-owed to create your account and prove your identity. When this is complete, you’ll be able to view and manage your repayments.
