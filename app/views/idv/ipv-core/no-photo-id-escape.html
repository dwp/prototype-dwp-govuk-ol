Find another way to prove your identity

If you do not have photo ID

You can try proving your identity with the service you need to use. They may take other types of ID.

If you have photo ID

If you have photo ID, you can prove your identity online by:

  • using the GOV.UK ID Check app
  • entering details from your UK passport or UK photocard driving licence and answering security questions

You can also prove your identity at a Post Office.

Types of photo ID you can use with the GOV.UK ID Check app and at the Post Office

For the GOV.UK ID Check app, you can use a:

  • UK photocard driving licence
  • UK passport
  • non-UK passport with a biometric chip
  • UK biometric residence permit (BRP)
  • UK biometric residence card (BRC)
  • UK Frontier Worker permit (FWP)

You can use an expired BRP, BRC or FWP up to 18 months after its expiry date with the GOV.UK ID Check app.

At a Post Office, you can use a:

  • UK photocard driving licence
  • European Union (EU) photocard driving licence
  • UK passport
  • non-UK passport
  • National identity card from a European Economic Area (EEA) country
How would you like to prove your identity?
You’ll be shown how to download and use the app.
