Prove your identity with GOV.UK One Login

You may need to prove your identity before you can use this government service.
You can do this with or without a photo ID.

With photo ID

If you have a photo ID, you can:

  • prove your identity online with a passport, driving licence or other form of accepted photo ID
  • prove your identity at a Post Office with a different form of photo ID, such as an EU driving licence or non-UK passport

Without photo ID

If you do not have photo ID, you can:

  • provide your bank or building society details
  • answer security questions - these will be about things like mobile phone contracts, bank accounts, credit cards, loans or mortgages you may have

You only need to prove your identity once

If you have proved your identity with GOV.UK One Login for a different service, you will not need to again.
