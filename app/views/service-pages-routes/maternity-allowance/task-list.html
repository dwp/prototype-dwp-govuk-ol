What happens next

We have sent you an SMS message to confirm your application. We will send you a letter when we have made a decision about your Maternity Allowance claim. We may call or write to you if we need more information.

If we have all the information we need, you should get a decision on your claim within 20 working days.

Changes you must report

You must tell us about changes in your circumstances that could affect your Maternity Allowance claim, for example:

  • if your baby is born early
  • if you go back to work early or use Keeping in Touch (KIT) days
  • if you start Shared Parental Leave, Statutory Shared Parental Pay or another benefit, or
  • if you go abroad (you do not need to tell us about holidays abroad)

To learn more about these and other changes and when you must tell us about them, visit www.gov.uk/report-benefits-change-circumstances
